Chicken Kofta Biryani

  • INGREDIENTS: For Koftas: Beef/ Chicken Mince ½ kg Bread Slices 2 Lemons 2 Onion s(finely chopped) 3 Black Pepper 4 Green Chilies 4 Green Cardamoms 4 Green Cardamoms 6 Yellow Food Color a pinch Oil 1 cup Fresh Milk 1 cup Rice 2 cups Yogurt ½ cup Mint Leaves ½ bunch Cumin Seeds 1 tsp Turmeric 1 tsp All Spice (ground) 1 tsp Red Chili Powder 1 tbsp Coriander (ground) 1 tbsp Chickpeas (roasted) 1 tbsp Poppy Seeds 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp Salt to taste For Kofta Green Masala: Mint Leaves ½ bunch Green Chilies (finely chopped) 4 METHODS Grind mince with 1 tbsp roasted chickpeas, 1 tbsp poppy seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds and black pepper. Now fold bread center soft part in the ground mince. Now grind mince again with green masala. Add in salt, green chilies and ½ bunch mint , mix well and make fine kofta balls. Shallow fry the mince balls. Now heat oil in a pan and fry onions till they turn golden brown, remove and spread them on paper. Crush these fried onions when they are dry and mix them with yogurt, add in red chilli powder, 1 tbsp ground coriander, 1 tsp turmeric and salt. Mix well and add this mixture in the pan and cook. Now spread fried kofta balls in the pan and leave it on dum for min. Boil 2 cups rice in a pan with salt, green chilies mint leaves , 6 green cardamoms. Cook till they are ¾ done Remove water from the rice. Now grease a pan and spread rice evenly on its base Now spread a layer of kofta masala, Spinkle mint leaves and green chilies Spread another layer of rice and pour yellow food color mixed with milk.
